# Big Talk — When Small Talk Ends, Big Talk Starts

## Problem
50,000 students. 300-person lectures. You sit next to someone every day and never discover you both think Evangelion's ending was genius. Small talk keeps us strangers.

## Solution
Big Talk uses Claude AI to find *surprising, non-obvious connections* between people — not just "you both like anime" but WHY your tastes reveal shared values, complementary worldviews, and genuine conversation-worthy bridges. Enter a room, share your interests, tap someone — and Big Talk gives you the perfect reason to actually talk.

## Why Now / Why Claude
This isn't a recommendation algorithm that traps you in a bubble. Claude's reasoning finds connections a matching algorithm never would — thematic bridges across anime, games, movies, and hot takes. It doesn't just match you — it tells you WHY you'd click.

## Demo Script (90 seconds)
1. (10s) "300-person lecture. You don't know the person next to you. What if you could?" — show landing page
2. (15s) Open on two phones. Both join room "CS577". Fill profiles quickly.
3. (10s) Room view — see each other's cards appear in real time.
4. (25s) Tap to match — show the animated reveal. Read the first connection out loud. "Claude found that you BOTH love stories about deeply flawed characters seeking redemption — and generated this question to get you talking."
5. (15s) Show the second match. Different people, different connections. "Every match is unique because Claude reasons about YOUR specific interests."
6. (15s) Show leaderboard. "The more you connect, the higher you climb. Big Talk rewards you for stepping outside your bubble."
